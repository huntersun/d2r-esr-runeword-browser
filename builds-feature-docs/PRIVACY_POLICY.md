# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We've kept it short and written in plain language because we believe you shouldn't need a lawyer to understand how your data is handled.

## Who We Are

This app is a free, open-source community tool for a Diablo 2 Resurrected mod. The source code is publicly available on GitHub under the MIT license. The app is maintained by an individual developer based in Hungary (EU).

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: **[YOUR_EMAIL_HERE]**

## What Data We Collect

### When you create an account

- **If you sign in with Discord:** We receive your Discord user ID, username, and avatar URL from Discord's OAuth service. We do not receive your Discord password or access to your Discord messages.
- **If you sign in with email magic link:** We store the email address you provide.
- **Display name:** You choose a public display name during registration. This is the only piece of identifying information visible to other users.

### When you use the app

- **Builds:** The character builds you create (item/runeword selections, build name, description, character class).
- **Likes:** Which builds you have liked.
- **Timestamp of your privacy policy acceptance.**

### What we do NOT collect

- We do not use analytics or tracking tools.
- We do not serve advertisements.
- We do not collect your IP address, device information, or location data for our own purposes.
- We do not use cookies for tracking. The app only uses session tokens necessary for authentication.

## Why We Collect This Data

All data we collect serves one purpose: to let you save your builds, share them with the community, and browse/like other players' builds. There is no secondary use.

| Data              | Purpose                                       |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Email / Discord ID | To authenticate your account                 |
| Display name       | To show who created a build                  |
| Build data         | To save and share your builds                |
| Likes              | To rank popular builds in the community      |

## What Is Publicly Visible

- Your **display name** and **public builds** are visible to all users (including unauthenticated visitors).
- Your **email address** is **never** visible to other users. It is stored in a separate, protected authentication system.
- Your **likes** are used for counting only — other users see the total like count on a build but cannot see a list of who liked it.

## Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on **Supabase**, a backend-as-a-service platform. Supabase stores data in managed PostgreSQL databases. The specific data center region will be noted here once the project is provisioned: **[REGION_HERE]**.

Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf. Their privacy practices are documented at [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

| Service   | Purpose                        | Data shared                             |
| --------- | ------------------------------ | --------------------------------------- |
| Supabase  | Database, auth, API hosting    | All account and build data              |
| Discord   | OAuth sign-in (if chosen)      | Discord user ID, username, avatar       |

We do not share your data with anyone else. We do not sell your data. We never will.

## Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

### Right to access

You can request a copy of all data we hold about you. Contact us at the email above, or view your data directly in the app (your profile, your builds, your likes).

### Right to rectification

You can update your display name at any time in the app's settings.

### Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)

You can delete your entire account from the app's settings. This permanently removes your profile, all your builds, all your likes, and your authentication record. This action is irreversible.

### Right to data portability

You can request an export of your data in a machine-readable format (JSON). Contact us at the email above.

### Right to withdraw consent

You can delete your account at any time, which revokes all consent and deletes all your data.

### Right to object

If you have concerns about how your data is being processed, contact us at the email above.

## Data Retention

- Your data is kept for as long as your account exists.
- When you delete your account, all associated data (profile, builds, likes) is permanently deleted immediately.
- We do not keep backups of deleted account data.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we will notify users through the app. The history of changes is available in the Git repository.
